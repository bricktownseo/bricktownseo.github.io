AdWords Terms

Ad Groups – Group of keywords, budget settings for an ad

Auction – The process of ranking the ads based on the user’s search terms each and every time a search is performed. For a little more detail on the auction process, below is a youtube video describing it more in detail.

Campaign – A collection of ad groups sharing similar settings

CPM – Cost per thousand clicks

CTR – Click through rate, the number of clicks versus impressions of your ads

Keywords – The words used to match a user search with your advertisement.
